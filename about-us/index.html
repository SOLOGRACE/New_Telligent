Telligent

Telligent Engineering Ltd is an Engineering Services & Technology Solutions Company with expertise in product development and systems integration for the Telecommunications, Oil & Gas, Power, Industrial and Offshore segments. We design, create, test, install, replace, retrofit and upgrade Electromechanical, Electrical, Telecommunication, Enterprise IT, Instrumentation & Automation systems (PLC, SCADA, Control System, IoT/IIoT and PAGA System).

We are a technology company geared towards providing best value and cost-effective solutions for multi-disciplinary projects while helping our customers do business better by leveraging our industry-wide experience, deep technology expertise, comprehensive portfolio of services and vertically aligned business models. Our dedicated staffs enable us to harness the latest technology for delivering business capability to our clients.
