Maritime

We enable monitoring capacity for strategic offshore activities.

Tracking for large and small marine vessels

We integrate a solution to maintain communication and display of large and small marine vessels in real-time, making logistics tasks more efficient, optimizing fuel consumption, safeguarding the physical integrity of the crew, and state of the catch or merchandise en route.

Benefits

Monitoring and management of the logistics of marine units using a reliable display platform

Crew safety while at sea

Reduction in rescue times in the event of contingencies at sea

Prevention of criminal activities in restricted offshore areas

Location of the unit in areas not covered by conventional signals (GPRS)
